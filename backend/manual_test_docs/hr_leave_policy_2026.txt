Title: HR Leave Policy 2026
Department: Human Resources

Annual Leave:
- Full-time employees receive 18 paid annual leave days per calendar year.
- Unused annual leave can be carried over, up to 5 days maximum.
- Carryover leave must be used by March 31 of the following year.

Sick Leave:
- Employees receive 10 paid sick leave days per year.
- A medical certificate is required for 3 or more consecutive sick days.

Remote Work:
- Employees may request up to 2 remote days per week with manager approval.
- Core collaboration hours are 10:00 to 16:00 local time.

Approval Process:
- Leave requests should be submitted at least 7 days in advance in the HR portal.
- Managers should approve or reject requests within 2 business days.

Policy Owner:
- HR Operations Team
- Effective Date: January 1, 2026
